Family Therapy

With sensitivity and compassion, I can help you find solutions to a wide range of life challenges and psychological issues. I work with each person to help you build on your strengths to identify and achieve your life goals.

I work with traditional and non-traditional families: married, cohabitating, single parent, or blended families. I support Parents and Friends of Lesbians and Gays, and welcome families with homosexual parents and/or children.

Family therapy is effective with just one individual or with couples, families or groups.

My Practice

  • Individual psychotherapy
    • Guiding individuals with relationship issues in all stages of life.
    • Certified in Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (www.EMDR.org) for Anxiety and Trauma
  • Couples counseling
    • Certified in Relationship Enhancement (RE) Couples Counseling (www.NIRE.org)
    • Combine EMDR with RE for couples with trauma history
  • Family therapy
    • All family members are invited to participate in creating the family they want to be.
    • Activities and games included to reach all ages of participants.
  • Highly recommended by professionals.
